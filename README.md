# 🛍️ Retail Analytics Project

### End-to-End Business Intelligence Solution using SQL, Python & Power BI

> An end-to-end retail analytics project that transforms raw operational data into actionable business insights using Python, SQL Server, and Power BI.

---

## ✨ Business Questions Answered

This project was built to answer key business questions that decision-makers face in a retail environment:

- 📈 How is sales performance changing over time?
- 🛍️ Which products and categories generate the highest revenue and profit?
- 👥 Who are the most valuable customers?
- 🎯 Which promotions drive the highest sales impact?
- ↩️ How do return rates affect overall business performance?
- 🌍 Which locations contribute the most to revenue and profitability?

# 📖 Project Overview

This project demonstrates a complete end-to-end retail analytics workflow, beginning with synthetic data generation in Python, followed by data preparation and business analysis in SQL Server, and culminating in interactive Power BI dashboards designed to support data-driven decision-making.

The analytics solution provides comprehensive visibility into sales performance, customer behavior, product performance, promotion effectiveness, return analysis, and geographic sales trends within a simulated retail business environment.

Rather than focusing solely on dashboard development, this project showcases the complete analytics lifecycle—from raw data generation to meaningful business insights.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Retail businesses generate thousands of transactions every day. Without a structured analytics system, decision-makers often struggle to monitor sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, product trends, promotion effectiveness, return rates, and regional sales performance.

The absence of integrated reporting makes it difficult to identify growth opportunities, evaluate marketing campaigns, understand customer behavior, and respond quickly to changing business conditions.

This project addresses those challenges by building a complete Business Intelligence solution that transforms raw operational data into interactive dashboards and actionable insights for better business decisions.
